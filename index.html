Title1

Please search for the desired certification on Lex. You may put a filter on format on the right navigator bar on Format as ‘Certification’. Open the certification and navigate to ‘Take Certification’ tab. There are three modes to register: a. Video Proctored Mode from anywhere. b. Booking slot at assessment center in your respective DC. c. Booking slot to take assessment from your cubicle.

Search for the relevant Role, Variant and Learning Path and complete all courses and at least one certification mentioned in the path. If you have 6 months of relevant experience, you are eligible for the tag. It is also subject to additional criteria set at the Org level.

Title2

Please search for the desired certification on Lex. You may put a filter on format on the right navigator bar on Format as ‘Certification’. Open the certification and navigate to ‘Certification’ tab. You will find the link to Upload Result. Fill in the required detailed and submit for verification approval.

Please get in touch with your Unit Competency Anchor or Unit HR.
